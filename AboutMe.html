Hey, how's it going? My name is Steve and I've been a part of the custom mechanical keyboard hobby since late 2018. What started off a little upgrade to my desk setup ended up spiraling into a rabbit hole of hours of research and binge watching videos. In the beginning of my journey, I owned a Razer keyboard with all the aformentioned features. The cheap plastic feeling and the incredibly loud switches left me wanting more. I found a video about a specific type of switch and that marked the beginning of the end. Before the start of covid, I had built my very first keyboard with the keycaps and switches that I had wanted, but as an old saying goes, there is no such thing as endgame. For those that are curious, this entire website was built using my Bakeneko60 (pictured on the home page) with the poly-fil mod, GMK Bingsu clones, TX stabs lubed with diaelectric grease, and Black Cherry Pie switches lubed with Krytox 205 and GPL 105 on 65g TX long springs. Now, I could break down what all of that means, but I would end up writing the equivalent of a 10 page essay. 

If you have any questions about the hobby feel free to send me a message at either my school or personal email.

Student Email: ssgonzalezmora@students.waketech.edu
Personal Email: stevegm772@gmail.com
