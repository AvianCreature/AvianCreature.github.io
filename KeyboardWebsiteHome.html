Custom Mechanical Keyboards
Home Page  | Intro  | How to Get Started 


Keyboards are a tool that millions of people all around the world use on a daily basis. Whether to type essays for school, code, or even conduct business and work matters, keyboards are a main component used to interact with computers. Most people spend the extra money to buy nicer phones, clothes, water bottles and so much more. So why shouldn't keyboards, something that's already used everyday, get the same treatment? 

The good majority of gamers and office space aficionados understand this concept all too well. However, for most people, their first nice keyboard is typically a fancy full size RGB keyboard with knobs and pillowy wrist rests. But what about beyond that? What if there's more to keyboards than cool lights and plastic shells? What if there's options that could fit your every need? The objective is to introduce as many people to the world of custom mechanical keyboards.
